Welcome to the Sport Climbing Association of the Philippines, Inc.

SCAPI is the national governing body of the sport of competition and outdoor climbing in the Philippines. As a non-profit, we promote three competition disciplines, bouldering, sport and speed climbing, through our series, as well as outdoor development projects around the country
